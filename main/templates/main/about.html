About

This website was, at first, designed as an experiment by a high school student interested in trying out the web framework Django, which used python for backend, a language he is adept in. The project was scaled to match his visions of creating a dedicated website for his personal interests. His primary, grand vision, however, is to help himself and others explore the seemingly complex and bizarre world of computer technology. This includes computer science topics such as Deep Learning, which the name of this website derives from.
